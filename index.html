Workloads everywhere!

We enable the execution of any type of workload in the cloud and at the edge combining open-source and custom components tailored to the application needs

Systems software

We have extensive experience running workloads as Virtual Machines on top of popular hypervisors such as Xen and QEMU/KVM. Moreover, we build on containers and use them to facilitate the deployment of multi-tier applications.

Lightweight execution

When possible, we optimize workload deployment by using lightweight systems software stacks, depending on the application needs. We achieve this with the use Amazon's Firecracker or unikernels while building on the concept of library operating systems.

Hardware Acceleration

We bridge the gap between hardware accelerators and virtualized workloads. We enable support for coarse-grained task acceleration by defining the simplest interface for common snippets of code that can be accelerated and transparently execute the code, in hardware, while the rest of the application execution flow is progressing.

Virtualization Research

We collaborate with academic institutions and R&D departments, to conduct systems research and move beyond the state-of-the-art on systems design and implementation.

Low-level systems development

Application porting

Deployment & Maintenance

We design, deploy, and maintain your application in various environments: the cloud, on-prem or at the edge. With more than 20 years of experience in systems administration, we relieve you from the burden of deploying, monitoring and maintaining your application.

Whether it's the cloud, on-prem or the Edge, we've got you covered!

At Nubis, we provide fast and reliable hosting services for your application, using our own infrastructure (on-prem servers or edge devices), your preferred infrastructure in our data center, or the Cloud (AWS EC2 & IBM). We setup and deploy your application in a reproducible way, and provide a detailed analysis of the deployment process. This prevents vendor lock-in so, at anypoint in time, you can deploy your application wherever you prefer!
